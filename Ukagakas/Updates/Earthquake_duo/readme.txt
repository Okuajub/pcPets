--Introduction--
Welcome! This is the Earthquake Recue Duo: Man-713 and Ridge. Together they save people in crisis from near certain doom! 
Man-713's strong robot body helps him carry people to safety and move obstacles! Ridge can rescue people from debris while Man-713 lifts it safely out of the way! 
As one unit, they work hard to protect the people of Japan from seismic activity!
